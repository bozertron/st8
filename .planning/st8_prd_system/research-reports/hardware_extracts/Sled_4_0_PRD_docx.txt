Product Description
Test system to validate acoustical arrangements for Chromatic advanced AI hearing aids.  Provides connectivity for up to 2 microphones and 1 speaker per side analog I/O, and USB interfacing on the other as a 4 channel in/2 channel out sound device.
Market Need
Internal use only
Key Features/ Functionality
Detachable cables for all components
Small, battery powered interface/basic processing box
Other Product Compatibility,
